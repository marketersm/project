Birth and childhood

The Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) was born in the Bani Hashim dynasty of the Quraish tribe in the city of Mecca in present day Saudi Arabia.
Hazrat Muhammad (PBUH) was born on Monday 12th Rabiul Awal in 570 AD. His mother's name is Amina and his father's name is Abdullah.
